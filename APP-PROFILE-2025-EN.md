1. Deep, thorough and complete understanding of the client's business operations;
2. Understand client's needs and propose appropriate consulting services;
3. Identify key risks/issues of the client's business model/operations;

5. Coordinate with client personnel to deploy and implement proposed solutions into business operations;
6. Monitor, check regularly & update new risks/issues with solutions.
