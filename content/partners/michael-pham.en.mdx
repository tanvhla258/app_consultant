Michael Pham has over 20 years of experience in audit, accounting, and advisory services, with a career spanning KPMG Vietnam, BDO, Mazars, and PKF. His experience across multiple international firms has given him a broad perspective on how different organizations approach financial reporting, risk, and compliance.

Michael has led engagements for some of Vietnam's most prominent companies and international investors, including Masan Consumer, Mapletree, Dragon Capital Group, and Mekong Capital. His sector expertise spans consumer goods, real estate, technology, financial services, and logistics.

Holding both ACCA and VACPA credentials, Michael brings the technical depth and professional standards that multinational clients and Vietnamese enterprises alike expect from a senior adviser.
